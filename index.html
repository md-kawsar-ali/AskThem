AskThem survey app

Create and participate in Surveys and experience
its often-astonishing results.

Question People Worldwide

Create Surveys and poll Users around the world. You can ask anything and anyone you like. You can choose your audience

Give Other Users Feedback

Participate in Surveys to see if your opinion differs or coincides with those of other users. You will help the Creators getting the Feedback they need.

Ask Users Worldwide or Followers Only

Decide whether your target group should consist of selected users around the world or Followers only. You have the choice.

Determine Your Target Group

Whether 100 users from Germany. 50 women from Pakistan or 200 randomly selected users worldwide. You choose your target group and determine its size.

Create Follower Surveys

Get the opinion of your closest friends and see how they think about certain topics. Whether it's about the upcoming party or the last math test.

Keep Track Of Your Feedback

In your profile you can check out how much feedback you have given so far and how much you have received. Try to keep your Feedback count balanced.
